Callum Wyllor's Must Watch List

My Top 3 Movies Of All Time

Welcome!

Thank you for coming to my page, this is a list of my favourite films across the years.

I wonder how many you'll agree with?

Number 1

The Dark Knight

An exceptional example of film making. This gritty tale about a fictional city under seige is only made better by the phenominal acting of Christian Bale and the late-great Heath Ledger.

Number 2

Avengers: Endgame

As a self-professed superhero fan, this is must-have on my list. A cinematographical wonder and a storytellers dream, this film is the culmination of over 10 years of and the peak of the Marvel Cinemic Universe.

Number 3

Star Wars Episode 1: The Phantom Menace

A controversial pick im sure, yet this film holds a special place in my heart as the first ever Star Wars film that I watched. This film introduced me to the Star Wars universe and stoked a fire within me that is still burning bright to this day.

And lets be honest, when Darth Maul first comes out with a double-bladed lightsabre, the film hit peak epicness!
